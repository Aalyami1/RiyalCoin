RiyalCoin Whitepaper

The official document that defines RiyalCoin’s vision, blockchain technology, and strategic roadmap within Saudi Vision 2030.

Vision

RiyalCoin aims to empower Saudi Arabia’s digital economy and foster financial innovation by introducing a cryptocurrency that reflects the nation’s values and cultural heritage while ensuring transparency and security through blockchain technology.

Mission

To provide a Saudi digital financial system built on trust, efficiency, and innovation — contributing to the goals of Vision 2030 by supporting digital transformation and the knowledge economy.

Technology

The project operates on a hybrid blockchain infrastructure combining public transparency with institutional oversight. It utilizes Proof of Stake (PoS) consensus for high efficiency, scalability, and low energy consumption.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 RiyalCoin
  • Initial Distribution: 40% Community, 25% Early Investors, 20% Strategic Partnerships, 10% Team, 5% National Reserve
  • Deflationary mechanism with scheduled token burns to maintain long-term value

Roadmap

2025: Launch of RiyalCoin testnet.

2026: Integration with governmental and private payment systems.

2027: Regional expansion and Gulf-wide adoption.

2028: Listing on international exchanges and partnerships with global institutions.

Alignment with Saudi Vision 2030

RiyalCoin aligns with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 by promoting digital financial innovation, supporting national investment initiatives, and establishing a sustainable, Sharia-compliant financial infrastructure for the future.
